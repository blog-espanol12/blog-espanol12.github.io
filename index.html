Our Mission

The environment is the main reason why we're able to surf and skate, therefore that is why we have a recycling program where you donate your old useless boards for a recycling process where you decide if you want something out of your old board, or if you just want to donate it for other purposes.

These purposes include donations to children in need that don't have the same opportunities that most of us have. We want to take surfing and skateboarding to another level. We want to help people in need to find happiness through surfing or skateboarding.
